About

Ashley Maximillian is a trans creative whose output consists of, and often combines, elements of digital and analogue visuals and sound. After a series of personal failures, she decided to dive into a passion she had explored on and off over the years: programming. Ashley got her first taste of coding when she took an Intro to Java class in high school. Later explorations included customizing a weapon pack for DOOM II and learning about Processing, a programming language created specifically for artistic expression
